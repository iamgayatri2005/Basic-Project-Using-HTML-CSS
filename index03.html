What's the difference?

  • visibility: hidden - The element is invisible but still occupies space in the layout.
  • display: none - The element is completely removed from the document flow and doesn't take up any space.

Notice how the middle box (🍿) is invisible but still maintains its space in the layout, while the third box (🍩) is completely gone!
